About Me

Abdul-Quadri Ayomide Al-Ameen, popularly known as Sen. MiideOba, is an indigene of Oke-Ogun, Oyo State, Nigeria, born into the Al-Ameen family on September 19. He began his education at Muslim Association of Nigeria (MAN) Primary School, Okeho, later completed primary in Lagos at Lad-Ikhlass, served as Social Prefect, and graduated from MAN Secondary School, Egbeda.

He is a recent graduate of Agricultural Extension and Rural Sociology (Olabisi Onabanjo University), finishing with Second-Class Upper (CGPA 4.02). He served as Class Rep, two-term OOUSU Senator, Coordinator of Departmental Association, Vice President of NAAS OOU, and 35th President of NAAS OOU. Also served as Vice President of the Leo Club and is a certified First-Tier Responder in Mental Health.

His leadership programs include NAAS Agro-Summit 2024, NAAS Ojude Faaji Cultural Festival, Freshers’ Week, NAAS Super League, Provost Cup, War Against Drug Abuse Seminar (with NDLEA), welfare projects, tutorials, and student security advocacy. He continues as an agri-tech enthusiast, writer, volunteer and speaker.

  • Campus role: 35th President, NAAS OOU
  • Degree: Agric. Extension & Rural Sociology (2:1, 4.02)
  • Interests: Agri-tech, Leadership, Writing
  • Certs: Mental Health Responder, Soft Skills (Jobberman)
